## Practical Guidance
- Build label schema for bounded cardinality.
- Avoid dynamic IDs as labels; keep details in log body fields.
- Narrow selectors early and avoid broad regex patterns.
- Use `drop`/`keep` pipeline stages to control parsed-field noise.
- Monitor stream count and query latency as first-class SLOs.
